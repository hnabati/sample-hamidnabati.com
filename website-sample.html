The Expo—Where Innovation Takes the Main Stage

See new technology in action and learn from seasoned experts at interactive sessions and activities. It’s all yours and it’s totally free at the ADM Expo Montréal.

From Innovations in 3D Printing to Cobots on the Factory Floor, See the Latest Products and Technologies Up Close

Our five-in-one manufacturing expo puts you in front of the industry’s top engineers, business leaders, and disruptive companies. Check the growing exhibitor list for ADM Expo Montréal and get ready to join a community of innovators driving design and manufacturing forward.

See Exhibitor List

Featured Activities at ADM Expo Montréal

Why limit yourself to reading about new equipment or solutions when you can explore them at the ADM Expo Montréal? See new equipment firsthand, network with hiring companies, and connect with seasoned experts all on one show floor.

Center Stage

Stop by for expert panel discussions, live demos, speaker presentations, and more at Center Stage on the expo floor. Sessions kick off every hour and are free for everyone.

Cannabis Packaging Tech Theatre

Set in a classroom format, the Cannabis Packaging Tech Theatre is a designated area for commercial suppliers to discuss their innovative packaging solutions for the promotionally limited Canadian market. Focus to include child resistance, branding, and packaging materials

Speed Networking

Back by popular demand. Make new connections on the fly at networking sessions dedicated to some of your favorite engineering topics.

Career Zone

Take your networking to the next level by connecting with hiring companies looking for your expertise. Stop by for free sessions, workshops, and networking activities.
